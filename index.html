Welcome

I am a PhD candidate at the Chair of Security Engineering, RUB, Bochum, Germany. I am studying side-channel attacks and security engineering under the supervision of Tim Güneysu.
In 2017, I completed a Master's Degree at Télécom SudParis, in telecommunication engineering, with a specialization in network and system security.

My current research interests are:

  • Side-channel attacks
  • Formal verification of masked implementations
